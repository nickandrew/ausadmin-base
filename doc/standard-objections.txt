Standard potential objections to a proposal
-------------------------------------------

There can be problems with:
 - the name
 - the topic
 - the proposal (formatting, etc)
 - other

Name problems:
- Group name does not fit namespace plan

Topic problems:
- Group topic already covered elsewhere
- Topic poorly defined

Proposal problems:
- Nonsense proposal
- Spelling errors and/or typos in proposal
- Group not justified - rationale insufficient

- Group will be abused by spammers
- Group will be abused by trolls / net.idiots
